about me

I first got introduced to software engineering in varsity, when I took a computer science course. I was immediately hooked by the challenge of solving problems using code. I also loved the creativity that was involved in software engineering. I could create anything I could imagine, from simple games to complex applications.

My love for math has always been a driving force in my software engineering career. I love the logical structure of mathematics, and I find that it helps me to think about problems in a more efficient way. I also love the challenge of solving mathematical problems using code.

I love writing front-end code because I enjoy creating beautiful and user-friendly interfaces. I also love solving back-end problems because I enjoy the challenge of working with data and algorithms.

I am passionate about software engineering, and I am excited to continue learning and growing in this field. I am confident that I can use my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact on the world.
